My Biography

Hello, My name is Stelios Klapsis and this website is a project assignment for my Game 111: Web Design and Database Fundamentals class. On this website you will find a basic implementation of standard html tags as well as some personal information about myself. If you prefer to jump to a short list of the projects I have worked on instead you can simply press HERE. Otherwise if you prefer to view my entire portfolio you can simply click on the face in the image above.

I was born in Quebec where I went through CEGEP and University later moving to Ontario to experience more of what Canada had to offer. I've worked a variety of jobs but I have always had a passion for programming and game developement. Without the benefit of a formalized education I started teaching myself how to code to create a foundation of knowledge. I began with HTML and CSS, later moving to the basic scripting languages Javascript and PHP. I then later moved on to some basic database work with mySQL. Before long I felt I had become proficient in their use and application however, that was not where my passion was, it was just a stepping stone. I began to teach myself the object oriented languages begining with C# which over time has become my primary programing language for all the implementations I could want. Armed with the experience of C# under my belt I began to teach myself Unity and that is when I realized just how interested I was in the field. Finally, all of the work I had put in began to pay off and I could build almost anything I could visualize. For myself, this was a dream come true. Since then I have build a variety of games and software, always in the search of my next great challenge. While not all of my work is open source I do have a lot of my open source work on my Github which you can find HERE.

As I began to plateau in my ability, I found myself interested in learning more. This led me to apply to the SLC Game Programming course in the hopes of finding a high quality formalized education in programming. Armed with a degree in this program I hope to one day find a job working in a stuido doing the thing I love more than anything else in the world - Making video games.

In my personal time I persue a wide variety of interests from game developement and programming to Youtube and video editing. I also have a blog that I use to occasionally share my thoughts on gaming. My YouTube channel acts as an extension of this as I post both a gaming related podcast and game reviews. You can CLICK HERE to go to my Blog or alternatively if you are interested in seeing the kind of YouTube content that I create you can find it HERE.

Nelson Mandela once said Courage is not the absence of fear, but the triumph over it. The brave man is not he who does not feel afraid, but he who conquers that fear. This quote resonates with me on a deep level and has in many ways been a guiding factor in my life. I may be an adult returning to college, having taken a path that in retrospect I had not hoped to go down. In doing so I have wasted a lot of time. However, this will not stop me from following my passion, no matter how afraid that makes me.

Games I have recreated

  1. Pacman
    • Pacman was the first game I attempted to recreated which proved to be a challenge. In retrospect I should have started with Pong.
  2. Pong
    • Pong was fairly easy to create given preivous experience but still a good learning opportunity.
  3. Mario
    • Maro was one of the more complex games from the NES era that I had ever worked on. It took considerable time to finish and polish.
  4. Galaga
    • Galaga is my favorite classic game and thought it took forever to finish I enjoyed every moment of it.
  5. Brick Breaker
    • Brick Breaker was an ideal game to learn how the physics system within Unity worked as well as the math needed to make it do what I wanted
  6. 2D Racers
    • I worked on a variety of differnt simple 2D racer games.
  7. 2D Top Down Space Shooters
    • This is a game I revisit time and again, perhaps it wormed its way into my heart as a child. Somehow they never seem to get old.
